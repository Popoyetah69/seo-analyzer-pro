## Critical Success Factors

### 1. Consistency Over Perfection
- Don't wait for "perfect" - launch when good enough
- Ship fast, iterate based on feedback
- 80% launch now beats 100% launch never

### 2. Focus on Revenue, Not Vanity Metrics
- Don't celebrate 1000 signups with 0 paying customers
- Optimize for conversion, not just traffic
- $1 in revenue > 100 free users

### 3. Personal Outreach Works
- Cold email with personalization: 3-5% reply rate
- Cold email without personalization: 0.5% reply rate
- Phone calls: 50%+ conversion once in conversation

### 4. Content Marketing is Compound
- Week 1: 1 blog post = 5 visitors
- Week 4: 3 blog posts = 50 visitors
- Month 3: 10 blog posts = 200 visitors

### 5. Network is Everything
- Tell EVERYONE you know about it
- Ask for introductions to people they know
- Personal recommendations = highest conversion

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Perfectionism** - Don't spend 3 months on "perfection"  
❌ **No marketing** - Build it, but they won't come automatically  
❌ **Wrong target** - Don't target "everyone" - target niche  
❌ **Price too high** - Better to underprice and over-deliver  
❌ **Slow support** - Reply to EVERY message within 2 hours  
❌ **No feedback loops** - Ask customers what they want
